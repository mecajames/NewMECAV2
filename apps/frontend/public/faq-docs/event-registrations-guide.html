📅 Event Registrations Guide

Managing your event registrations and signing up for competitions

Registration List

The Event Registrations tab shows all events you've registered for, with key details at a glance:

The event registrations list with status indicators

ColumnWhat It Shows
Event NameThe name of the competition event
DateWhen the event takes place
LocationVenue city and state
FormatCompetition format (SPL, SQL, Show & Shine, Ride the Light)
ClassYour registered competition class
StatusRegistration status (Confirmed, Pending, Cancelled)

Using Filters

When you have many registrations, use the filters to find what you need:

Filter controls for narrowing down your registrations

  • Search — Type an event name or location to quickly find a specific registration
  • Season — Filter by competition season to view registrations from a specific year
  • Country — Filter by event country
  • State — Filter by event state/province
💡 Tip: Filters can be combined. For example, select "2025" season and "Texas" state to see only your Texas events from 2025.

Registration Statuses

Each registration has one of these statuses:

StatusMeaningWhat to Do
Confirmed Your registration is complete and confirmed You're all set! Show up on event day ready to compete.
Pending Registration received but awaiting confirmation Payment may be processing, or the event director needs to approve. Check back soon.
Cancelled Registration has been cancelled The event may have been cancelled, or you cancelled your registration.

Viewing Event Details

Click on any registration to view the full event details:

  • Complete event schedule and timeline
  • Venue address with map link
  • Competition formats and classes offered
  • Event director and contact information
  • Other registered competitors (if publicly visible)
  • Entry fees and payment details

Finding & Registering for Events

Ready to sign up for a new competition?

1
Visit the Events page from the main navigation.
2
Browse upcoming events or use filters to find events near you.
3
Click on an event to view its details, formats, and registration information.
4
Click "Register" and select your competition format and class.
5
Complete payment if required. Your registration will appear in the Event Registrations tab.
Requirements: You must have an active MECA membership to register for competitions. If you don't have one, you'll be prompted to purchase a membership during registration.

Tips for Competition Day

  • Arrive early — Check-in usually opens 30-60 minutes before the event starts
  • Bring your card — Have your membership card ready (phone or printed) for check-in
  • Know your class — Confirm your registered class and format before arriving
  • Check the rules — Review the Rulebooks for your competition format
  • Test your system — Make sure your audio setup is working properly before the event
💡 Tip: Not sure which class to register for? Use the Class Calculator to determine your SPL class based on your vehicle and equipment setup.
