1. Profile Hub Overview

When you click the Profile tab in My MECA, you'll see a hub with three cards. Click any card to open the matching page. From any of those pages you can also use the dropdown selector in the top-right of the first card to switch between the three views without going back to the hub.

The Profile hub with cards for each section

Card What It Contains
Member & Billing Profile Name, email, phone, address, vehicle info, MECA ID, membership status
Public Profile Profile visibility toggle, competitor name, audio system, vehicle info
Gallery Upload, manage, and reorder your competition photos (up to 6). Set any photo as your profile picture. (Gallery Guide)
💡 Tip: Payments & Invoices, My Memberships, Shop Orders, and Event Registrations have moved to their own dashboard tab — the new Membership & Billing hub. Support Tickets is now its own dashboard tab as well.

2. Member & Billing Profile

Your Member & Billing Profile contains the core information tied to your MECA account — the same info used on your invoices and receipts.

Editing Your Member & Billing Profile

1
Go to My MECA → Profile tab → click "Member & Billing Profile" card.
2
Click the "Edit Profile" button (pencil icon) at the top of the page to enter edit mode.
3
Update any of the fields listed below.
4
Click "Save Changes" to save. Your changes take effect immediately.
💡 Tip: The Profile View dropdown in the top-right of the Profile Information card lets you jump straight to the Public Profile or Gallery without going back to the hub.

The Profile View dropdown — switches between Member & Billing Profile, Public Profile, and Gallery

Updating Your Profile Photo

Your profile photo is set from the Profile Images section on your Public Profile page. Upload images there (up to 5), then click on any image to set it as your profile picture. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP. Max 5 MB per image. See the Gallery Guide for full details.

3. Public Profile

Your public profile is what other MECA members see when they visit your profile page in the member directory.

What's on Your Public Profile Page

The Public Profile page has several sections you can manage:

  • Profile Visibility — Toggle whether your profile appears in the Member Directory
  • Competitor Information — Your competitor name as it appears in results and standings
  • Audio System — Free-form description of your competition build
  • Vehicle info — Your competition vehicle details (read-only here — edit on the Member & Billing Profile)

Photos & Profile Picture

Photos are now managed on the dedicated Gallery page, not on the Public Profile page. Use the dropdown in the top-right of the Public Profile card to switch to the Gallery view, or open it from Profile → Gallery card. See the Gallery Guide for full instructions.

Competitor Name

Your Competitor Name is the name that appears in competition results, standings, and Top 10 lists. You can change it from the Public Profile page.

💡 Tip: A complete public profile with a bio and photos helps you connect with other competitors and makes your profile stand out in the member directory.

4. Payments & Invoices

Payments & Invoices is now reached from the Membership & Billing tab on My MECA, then clicking the My Payments & Invoices card. The page has tabs across the top — Overview, Memberships, Shop Orders, Event Registrations, Invoices — so you can filter the list by purchase type. Clicking View on any row opens an in-app receipt or invoice viewer; that viewer has a Print / Download button that opens a clean printable version in a new tab.

The Membership & Billing hub with four cards

Payments & Invoices with the Current Membership card and tab navigation

In-app invoice viewer — Print / Download opens the printable version in a new tab

Membership Receipt viewer — used for $0 / complimentary memberships that have no formal invoice

The Billing & Invoices page with payment history

Viewing Payment History

Your Billing page shows a list of all payments made through MECA, including:

  • Membership purchases and renewals
  • Event registration fees
  • Date and amount for each transaction
  • Payment status (Paid, Pending, Refunded)

Viewing Invoices

Click on any payment to view or download the invoice as a PDF. Invoices include:

  • Transaction details (date, amount, description)
  • Payment method used
  • MECA billing information

Managing Payment Methods

Click "Manage Billing" to open the Stripe billing portal where you can:

  • Add or update your credit/debit card
  • View upcoming charges
  • Download invoices
  • Update your billing address
💡 Tip: The Stripe billing portal is a secure external page hosted by Stripe. Your payment information is never stored on MECA servers.

5. My Memberships

To manage your membership, go to My MECA → Membership & Billing → My Memberships. From there you can view your current plan, secondary memberships, membership history, and your digital membership card.

The Membership section showing your current membership details

Viewing Your Membership

Your membership card shows:

  • Membership type and category (Competitor, Competitor w/Team, Retailer, Manufacturer)
  • Status (Active, Expired, Cancelled)
  • Start and expiration dates
  • Auto-renewal status

Upgrading Your Membership

1
Go to My MECA → Membership & Billing → My Memberships.
2
Click "Renew Membership" on your active plan.
3
Select your new membership type.
4
Complete payment. Your upgrade takes effect immediately.

Auto-Renewal

When auto-renewal is enabled, your membership will automatically renew before it expires using the payment method on file. You can toggle auto-renewal on/off from the My Memberships page.

Cancelling Your Membership

To cancel your membership:

  • Go to My MECA → Membership & Billing → My Memberships
  • Click "Cancel Membership"
  • Confirm the cancellation in the dialog
⚠ Warning: Cancelling your membership means you won't be able to compete in MECA events or earn points. Your MECA ID and account data are preserved — you can always renew later.

6. Support Tickets

Support Tickets has been promoted to its own dashboard tab. Click the Support tab in My MECA to open your tickets dashboard, or visit /tickets directly.

Creating a Ticket

1
Click the Support tab in My MECA, or visit /tickets.
2
Click "New Ticket".
3
Select a category (Account, Membership, Event, Technical, etc.).
4
Write a detailed description of your issue. Include your MECA ID and any relevant screenshots.
5
Click "Submit". You'll receive an email confirmation.

Tracking Your Tickets

Each ticket has a status:

StatusMeaning
OpenYour ticket has been submitted and is waiting for a response
In ProgressA support team member is working on your request
ResolvedYour issue has been addressed
ClosedThe ticket is complete
💡 Tip: Include as much detail as possible in your ticket — screenshots, error messages, steps to reproduce — for the fastest resolution.

7. Business Listing (Retailers & Manufacturers)

If you have a Retailer or Manufacturer membership, you'll see an additional "Business Listing" card in your Profile hub.

What's Included

  • Business name and description
  • Contact information — Phone, email, website
  • Address and location — For map display in the directory
  • Logo and photos
  • Services offered

Your business listing appears in the appropriate directory (Retailer or Manufacturer) on the MECA website, helping customers find you.

Editing Your Listing

Click "Business Listing" in the Profile hub, then click "Edit" to update any of the fields. Changes are published immediately after saving.

💡 Tip: A complete listing with logo, photos, and detailed description significantly increases visibility in the directory.
