1. About Teams

MECA teams allow competitors to band together, representing a shop, brand, or group at competitions. Teams provide:

  • Group identity — A team name, logo, and profile visible in the directory
  • Member organization — Keep track of your team roster with roles and permissions
  • Combined results — See how your team performs across events
  • Team gallery — Showcase your team's builds and competition photos

2. Creating a Team

1
Go to My MECA → Team tab.
2
Click "Create Team".
3
Fill in the team details:
  • Team Name — Choose a unique name for your team
  • Team Type — Competition, Shop, or Brand
  • Description — A short bio about your team
  • Max Members — Set the maximum roster size
  • Join Approval — Choose whether new members need approval or can join freely
4
Click "Create". You'll automatically become the team Owner.

The team creation form (top section)

The team creation form (bottom section)

💡 Tip: Enable join approval if you want to review and approve members before they join. This is recommended for competition teams to maintain quality control.

3. Joining a Team

Browsing Available Teams

From the Team tab, you can browse existing teams and request to join:

  • View team profiles, member counts, and descriptions
  • Click "Request to Join" on any team
  • If the team requires approval, your request will be reviewed by team leadership
  • If the team has open joining, you'll be added immediately

Accepting an Invitation

Team owners and moderators can invite you by your MECA ID. When invited:

  • You'll see a notification in your dashboard
  • Go to the Team tab to accept or decline the invitation
  • Accepting adds you to the team immediately

5. Managing Members

Inviting by MECA ID

The invite member dialog where you enter a MECA ID

1
Open your team page and click "Invite Member".
2
Enter the person's MECA ID number.
3
The system will look up the member and show their name for confirmation.
4
Click "Send Invite". They'll receive a notification to accept or decline.

Approving Join Requests

If your team requires approval, pending requests appear on the team page. Click "Approve" or "Deny" for each request.

Changing Roles

Owners and Co-Owners can change member roles:

  • Click on a member's name in the roster
  • Select the new role from the dropdown
  • Confirm the change

Removing Members

Owners and Co-Owners can remove members from the team. Click the remove button next to the member's name and confirm.

⚠ Warning: Removing a member is immediate. They'll need to request to rejoin if they want to come back.

6. Team Gallery & Settings

Team Gallery

All team members can upload photos to the team gallery. These photos appear on the team's public profile page. Upload works the same as personal gallery uploads — click the upload area and select images.

Editing Team Settings

Owners and Co-Owners can edit:

  • Team name and description
  • Team logo/avatar
  • Join approval mode (open vs. approval required)
  • Maximum member count

7. Advanced Team Management

Transferring Ownership

As the team Owner, you can transfer ownership to another member:

  • Click the member you want to make the new Owner
  • Select "Transfer Ownership"
  • Confirm the transfer — you'll become a Co-Owner

Leaving a Team

Any member (except the Owner) can leave a team at any time from the team page. Click "Leave Team" and confirm.

Deleting a Team

Only the Owner can delete a team. This permanently removes the team, its gallery, and all member associations. This action cannot be undone.

Multiple Teams

You can be a member of multiple teams simultaneously. Use the team switcher at the top of the Team tab to switch between your teams.

💡 Tip: If you're in multiple teams, your competition results are attributed to you individually — team affiliation doesn't affect scoring.
