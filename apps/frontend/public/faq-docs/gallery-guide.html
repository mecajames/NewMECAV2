📷 Photo Gallery Guide

Viewing, uploading, and managing your competition photos

Gallery Overview

The Gallery page is your dedicated photo manager. Open it from the Profile tab on My MECA by clicking the Gallery card, or use the Profile View dropdown in the top-right of the Member & Billing Profile / Public Profile pages and pick Gallery. These photos appear on your public profile in the member directory.

The gallery grid view showing your uploaded photos

Viewing Your Gallery

  • Photos are displayed in a responsive grid (2 columns on mobile, up to 4 on desktop) using the same square thumbnail style as team galleries
  • Click any photo to view it full size in a lightbox
  • Hover over a photo to reveal action buttons: Set as profile picture and Remove

Uploading Photos

You can upload up to 6 images. Changes save automatically — no Save button needed.

1
Go to My MECA → Profile tab → "Gallery" card. (Or visit /member-profile-gallery directly.)
2
Click the dashed Upload tile in the grid (or the "Upload First Photo" button if your gallery is empty).
3
Select an image from your file picker. The image uploads and saves automatically.

The Gallery page with the upload tile and uploaded photos

Setting Your Profile Picture

Hover over any uploaded photo and click the green check button to set it as your profile picture. The selected image is marked with a "Profile" badge and an orange border. Your profile picture appears on your membership card, in the member directory, and next to your name throughout the site.

Photo Requirements

RequirementDetails
FormatsJPG, PNG, GIF, WebP (any image type)
Max file size5 MB per image
Max images6 per profile
ContentVehicle photos, competition shots, build progress, audio equipment

Managing Photos

Removing Photos

To remove a photo:

  • Open the Gallery page
  • Hover over the photo you want to remove
  • Click the red X button that appears on the image
  • The photo is deleted from your gallery immediately (no Save needed)
Note: Removing a photo deletes it permanently from your gallery and your public profile. If it was set as your profile picture, that selection is cleared too. This action cannot be undone.

Tips for Great Competition Photos

  • Show your setup — Take photos of your audio system, trunk build, and speaker placement
  • Good lighting — Natural daylight or well-lit areas produce the best results
  • Multiple angles — Capture front, side, and detail shots of your build
  • Competition moments — Photos from events, with trophies, or during judging make great additions
  • Build progress — Documenting your build journey is a great way to showcase your work
💡 Tip: Members with detailed photo galleries often attract more attention in the member directory and get more engagement from the community.
