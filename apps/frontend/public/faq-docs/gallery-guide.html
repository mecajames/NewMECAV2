📷 Photo Gallery Guide

Viewing, uploading, and managing your competition photos

Gallery Overview

The Gallery tab in your My MECA dashboard displays all photos you've uploaded to your profile. These are the same images shown in your Profile Images section on the Public Profile page, and they're also visible to other members on your public profile in the member directory.

Viewing Your Gallery

  • Photos are displayed in a responsive grid layout
  • Click any photo to view it full size in a new tab
  • The Gallery tab is read-only — to add or remove photos, click the "Manage Photos" button which takes you to your Public Profile

Uploading Photos

Photos are managed through the Profile Images section on your Public Profile page. You can upload up to 5 images.

1
Go to My MECA → Profile tab → "Public Profile" card.
2
Scroll down to the "Profile Images" section.
3
If you have fewer than 5 images, you'll see an empty slot with an Upload icon. Click it to open your file picker and select an image.
4
The image uploads automatically. It will appear in both the Profile Images grid and your Gallery tab.

Setting Your Profile Picture

Click on any uploaded image in the Profile Images grid to set it as your profile picture. Your profile picture appears on your membership card, in the member directory, and next to your name throughout the site.

Managing Photos

Removing Photos

To remove a photo from your profile:

  • Go to Profile → "Public Profile"
  • Scroll to the Profile Images section
  • Hover over the photo you want to remove
  • Click the X button that appears on the image
Note: Removing a photo deletes it from both your Profile Images and your Gallery tab. This action cannot be undone.

Tips for Great Competition Photos

  • Show your setup — Take photos of your audio system, trunk build, and speaker placement
  • Good lighting — Natural daylight or well-lit areas produce the best results
  • Multiple angles — Capture front, side, and detail shots of your build
  • Competition moments — Photos from events, with trophies, or during judging make great additions
  • Build progress — Documenting your build journey is a great way to showcase your work
💡 Tip: Members with detailed photo galleries often attract more attention in the member directory and get more engagement from the community.
