1. Creating Your Account

Getting started with MECA takes just a few minutes:

1
Visit mecausa.com and click "Sign Up" in the top navigation bar.
2
Enter your email address and create a secure password. You can also sign up with Google for faster access.
3
Check your email for a verification link. Click the link to confirm your account. Be sure to check your spam folder if you don't see it.
4
Complete your basic profile — your first name, last name, and phone number. You can fill in more details later.

2. Your MECA ID

Once you purchase a membership, you'll receive a unique MECA ID — a number that identifies you across all MECA competitions and services.

Your MECA ID is displayed in the dashboard header

Upload as: meca-id-header.png • Title: MECA ID in Dashboard Header • Desc: Dashboard header bar showing the MECA ID badge next to the member name • Tags: faq, getting-started, meca-id, dashboard, header

Where to Find Your MECA ID

  • Dashboard header — Displayed next to "MECA ID:" at the top of your My MECA page
  • Membership Card — Printed on your digital membership card
  • Profile page — Shown in your Personal Profile section

What Is It Used For?

  • Event check-in and registration confirmation
  • Team invitations (other members can invite you by MECA ID)
  • Competition result tracking across seasons
  • Support ticket identification
Your MECA ID is permanent. It stays with you for the lifetime of your membership, even if you change membership types or let your membership lapse and renew later.

3. Choosing a Membership

MECA offers several membership types to fit your needs:

Membership Categories

  • Competitor — For individuals who compete in MECA events (SPL, SQL, Show & Shine, Ride the Light)
  • Competitor w/Team — For competitors who wish to own an officially recognized Team within the association
  • Retailer — For car audio shops and retailers who want directory listings and event sponsorship
  • Manufacturer — For product manufacturers in the mobile electronics industry

The membership type selection page

Upload as: membership-selection.png • Title: Membership Type Selection • Desc: Membership selection page showing Competitor, Competitor w/Team, Retailer & Manufacturer cards with pricing • Tags: faq, getting-started, membership, selection, pricing

How to Purchase

1
Go to the Membership page from the main navigation bar.
2
Browse the available membership types and select the one that fits your needs.
3
Click "Get Started" on your chosen membership to go to the checkout page.
4
Complete payment via Stripe (credit/debit card). Your membership is activated immediately and you'll receive your MECA ID.
💡 Tip: Memberships run on an annual cycle. You can enable auto-renewal in your Profile → Membership section to avoid lapses.

4. Setting Up Your Profile

A complete profile helps other members connect with you and ensures smooth competition experiences.

Essential Profile Information

  • Profile Photo — Upload a clear photo for your public profile and membership card
  • Personal Info — Name, email, phone, city/state/country
  • Vehicle Information — Year, make, model of your competition vehicle
  • Bio — A short description about yourself for the member directory

For a complete walkthrough, see the Profile & Account Guide.

6. Your First Competition

Ready to compete? Here's how to get registered:

1
Find an event — Visit the Events page to browse upcoming competitions near you.
2
Check the details — Review the event date, location, formats offered, and registration deadline.
3
Register online — Click "Register" on the event page. Select your competition class and format.
4
Prepare your vehicle — Make sure your audio system is ready and your vehicle meets class requirements.
5
Show up and compete! — Arrive at the venue on time, check in with your MECA ID or membership card, and have fun.
Not sure which class? Use the Class Calculator to determine your SPL class, or check the Rulebooks for detailed class definitions.

7. Getting Help

If you run into any issues or have questions, MECA has multiple support channels:

  • Support Tickets — The best way to get help. Go to Member Support and click "Open Support Ticket." Our team typically responds within 24–48 hours.
  • Phone — Call 615-851-PHAT (7428) for urgent matters during business hours.
  • This Help Center — Browse our guides for step-by-step answers.
💡 Tip: When submitting a support ticket, include your MECA ID and as much detail as possible (screenshots help!) for the fastest resolution.
