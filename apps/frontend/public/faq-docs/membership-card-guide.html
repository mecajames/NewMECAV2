Viewing Your Card

To view your membership card:

1
Go to My MECA dashboard.
2
Click the "Membership Card" tab in the navigation bar.
3
Your card will be displayed with all your membership information.
No card showing? The Membership Card tab only displays a card if you have an active membership. If you see a prompt to get a membership, you'll need to purchase one first — see the Membership section of the Profile Guide.

Printing Your Card

You can print your membership card to carry a physical copy to events:

1
Navigate to the Membership Card tab.
2
Click the "Print Card" button below the card.
3
Your browser's print dialog will open with the card formatted for printing.
4
Select your printer and print. For best results, use card stock or thick paper.
💡 Tip: For the best print quality, use the "Fit to page" or "Scale to 100%" option in your printer settings. Printing on card stock gives a more professional feel.

Using Your Card at Events

You can present your membership card at MECA competitions for check-in. There are two ways:

Option 1: Phone Display

  • Open the Membership Card tab on your phone's browser
  • Show the card to event staff at check-in
  • They can scan the QR code or verify your MECA ID manually

Option 2: Printed Copy

  • Bring your printed card to the event
  • Present it at the registration desk
  • The QR code and MECA ID work the same as the digital version
Either method is accepted at all MECA events. Some competitors prefer the convenience of their phone, while others like having a physical card. Both are equally valid.

About the QR Code

The QR code on your membership card:

  • Links directly to your public MECA member profile
  • Can be scanned by any standard QR code reader or phone camera
  • Allows event staff to quickly verify your membership status
  • Shows your membership type, status, and expiration date
💡 Tip: The QR code is unique to your account and updates automatically if your membership changes. You don't need to reprint your card unless you want the latest expiration date on the physical copy.

Troubleshooting

Card Not Showing

If you don't see a membership card on the Membership Card tab:

  • No active membership — You need an active membership to generate a card. Visit Profile → Membership to purchase one.
  • Membership expired — Your card disappears when your membership expires. Renew to get it back.
  • Just purchased — If you just bought a membership, try refreshing the page. It should appear within a few seconds.

QR Code Not Scanning

  • Make sure your screen brightness is turned up
  • Hold the phone steady and ensure the full QR code is visible
  • If on a printed card, make sure the print is clear and not smudged
