1. Stats Cards

At the top of your Overview tab, you'll see four summary cards that give you a quick snapshot of your MECA activity:

The four stats cards at the top of the Overview tab

Card What It Shows
Membership Status Your current membership status (Active, Expired, or None). Shows your membership type name and expiry date if active.
Events Total number of events you've registered for across all seasons.
Total Points Your cumulative competition points earned from placements. Points are awarded for top-5 finishes.
Best Placement Your highest placement across all competitions (e.g., "1st Place").
💡 Tip: If your stats seem off, it may be because results from a recent event haven't been published yet. Results are typically posted within a few days after an event.

2. Membership Banner

If you don't have an active membership, you'll see a prominent banner encouraging you to get one. This banner shows:

  • Why a membership is important (required to compete, earn points, get a MECA ID)
  • A direct link to purchase a membership

Once you have an active membership, this banner disappears and is replaced by your membership type badge in the dashboard header.

3. Quick Action Cards

Below the stats, you'll find shortcut cards to the most common tasks:

Quick action cards for common tasks

  • Manage Profile — Jump to your Profile tab to update personal info
  • Gallery — Go to your photo gallery
  • Team — Access team management
  • Support — Open a support ticket or visit Member Support
  • Help — Come back to this Help Center

These cards are just shortcuts — you can always access the same sections via the tab bar at the top of the dashboard.

4. Notifications

The Notifications panel shows important updates about your account and activities:

The notifications panel with unread and read notifications

Types of Notifications

  • Event updates — Changes to events you're registered for
  • Result postings — When your competition results are published
  • Team activity — Join requests, role changes, and team updates
  • Membership reminders — Renewal and expiration notices
  • System announcements — Important MECA news and updates

Managing Notifications

  • Mark as read — Click the checkmark icon on any notification to mark it as read
  • Delete — Click the trash icon to remove a notification
  • Bell icon — The bell icon in the dashboard header shows your unread count

5. Recent Activity

The bottom of the Overview tab shows your most recent activity:

Recent registrations and competition results

Recent Registrations

Your latest event registrations with status indicators (Confirmed, Pending, Cancelled). Click on any registration to see full event details.

Recent Results

Your most recent competition results showing event name, class, score, placement, and points earned. Placement badges (gold, silver, bronze) make it easy to spot your best finishes.

6. Judge & Event Director Sections

If you have special roles (Judge or Event Director), additional sections appear on your Overview tab:

Judge Section

  • Your judge profile status and certification level
  • Upcoming judging assignments
  • Link to judge tools and resources

Event Director Section

  • Events you're directing
  • Upcoming event assignments
  • Quick links to event management tools
💡 Tip: These sections only appear if your account has been assigned judge or event director roles by a MECA administrator.

7. Finals Voting

During voting periods (typically leading up to the MECA Finals), a special voting section appears on your dashboard:

  • See the current voting status (open, closed, upcoming)
  • Cast your votes for Finals categories
  • View your submitted votes

Voting is only available to members with active memberships during designated voting periods.
