1. Account & authentication

EmailRecipientTrigger
Account verificationNew memberSign-up. Member clicks link to confirm email address before account is fully active.
Password set / resetMemberAdmin reset of their password, or member-initiated "Forgot Password". Includes secure link or temporary password.
Force password change noticeMemberAdmin set force_password_change = true. Member is required to change on next login.

2. Membership lifecycle

EmailRecipientTrigger
Membership WelcomePrimary memberMembership purchase completes; MECA ID assigned.
Secondary Member WelcomeSecondary memberPrimary added a secondary; the secondary gets their own welcome with their MECA ID.
Renewal ConfirmationMemberStripe successfully charged the auto-renewal.
Membership ExpiringMember30 days before endDate, sent by the daily cron.
Membership ExpiredMemberDay after endDate; member is now inactive.
Membership Cancelled / RefundedMemberAdmin issued a refund (full or partial). Includes refund amount and reason.
Subscription CancelledMemberAuto-renew was cancelled (cancel-at-renewal or member self-service).
Payment Failed (Dunning)MemberAuto-renew charge failed. Day 1 / 3 / 7 / 14 (auto-suspend) escalation.

3. Payment, refund, invoice

EmailRecipientTrigger
Invoice (PDF attached)MemberManually generated by admin or auto-issued for memberships, registrations, shop orders.
Invoice Auto-CancelledMemberUnpaid invoice past its grace window — system auto-cancelled it.
Invoice OverdueMemberSent a few days before auto-cancellation as a final reminder.
Refund ConfirmationMemberAny non-membership refund (e.g. event registration refund). Membership-specific refunds use the Membership Cancelled / Refunded template.

4. Events

EmailRecipientTrigger
Event Registration ConfirmationMemberSuccessful event registration with classes selected.
Event Registration CancelledMemberAdmin or member cancelled their registration.
Event ReminderMemberSent 7 / 3 / 1 day before the event by the daily cron.
Event Interest ReminderGuestGuest expressed interest in an event but didn't register; reminded as event approaches.
Guest Interest VerificationGuestGuest signed up to "follow" an event. Click link to verify email.
Event Rating RequestMemberSent the day after an event the member attended; asks them to rate their judges and ED.
World Finals InvitationQualified memberMember ranks in the season top-N; invite to register for World Finals.
World Finals QualificationMemberConfirmation that they qualified.
