Family & Team Memberships

Your Guide to Managing Multiple MECA Memberships

Do you have family members or team members who want to compete?

With MECA's Family & Team Membership feature, you can manage multiple memberships from a single account. Each person gets their own unique MECA ID and can compete independently, while you handle all the billing in one place!

Perfect for:

  • Parents managing memberships for their children
  • Spouses who both compete
  • Team sponsors covering memberships for competitors
  • Businesses paying for employee memberships

How It Works

üè† Master Account

When you add your first family/team member, your account becomes a "Master Account". As the master, you:

  • Handle billing for all linked memberships
  • Add new family/team members (up to 10)
  • Switch between MECA IDs to see different members' info
  • Register family members for events (if they don't have their own login)

üë• Linked Members

Each person you add becomes a "linked member" with:

  • Their own unique MECA ID - for competition tracking
  • Their own competition history - results tied to their ID
  • Their own profile - name, vehicle info, etc.
  • Optional: Their own login - so they can access the system too
Each linked member keeps their MECA ID forever - even if they later get their own independent account!

Adding a Family or Team Member

1
Go to Your Dashboard
Log in to your MECA account and click on My MECA in the navigation.
2
Find Your Membership
Scroll down to the My Memberships section. You'll see your current membership listed.
3
Click "Add Family/Team Member"
Look for the Add Family/Team Member button next to your membership. Click it to start the wizard.
4
Enter Their Information
Fill in the new member's details:
  • Competitor Name - How they'll appear in results
  • Vehicle Info - Make, model, color, license plate
  • Team Name (optional) - If they're part of a team
5
Choose Login Option
Decide if they need their own login (see next section for details):
  • No login - You manage everything for them
  • Give them a login - They can access the system independently
If giving them a login, you'll need to provide their email address.
6
Select Membership Type
Choose which type of membership they need (usually the same as yours).
7
Review & Pay
Review all the information and complete the payment. Once paid, they'll receive their unique MECA ID!

Which Should You Choose?

  • No Login: Best for young children or members who don't need system access
  • With Login: Best for older children, spouses, or anyone who wants to check their own results or register for events themselves
Members with their own login can see their competition results and register for events, but they cannot see invoices, payment information, or add new memberships. All billing stays with you.

Using the MECA ID Switcher

  1. Click the dropdown - Shows all MECA IDs you control
  2. Select a MECA ID - Dashboard updates to show that person's info
  3. View their stats - See competition results, points, etc.
  4. Take actions - Register for events, edit profile (if no login)
Your own MECA ID will be marked as "Primary" in the dropdown. Family/team members will show as "Secondary".

Understanding Your Billing

üìß Separate Invoices

Each family/team member has their own membership dates. When it's time to renew, you'll receive separate invoices for each person. This lets you choose whether to renew each membership individually.

üí≥ Single Payment Account

When a Member Wants Their Own Account

Sometimes a family or team member may want to have their own fully independent account - for example, when a child turns 18 or a sponsored competitor moves on.

What Happens When They Become Independent:

  • ‚úì They keep their same MECA ID
  • ‚úì They keep all their competition history
  • ‚úì Billing transfers to their own account
  • ‚úì They get full account access (billing, adding memberships, etc.)
Once a member becomes independent, you will no longer be responsible for their billing. They'll need to pay for their own renewals going forward.

To make a member independent, please contact MECA support or an administrator.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many family/team members can I add?
You can add up to 10 family or team members to your account.
Can I add members with different membership types?
Yes! Each family/team member can have a different membership type. For example, you could have a Competitor membership while adding a family member with a different tier.
What if I forget to renew a family member's membership?
Each membership has its own renewal date. You'll receive reminder emails before each one expires. If you don't renew, that specific membership will expire but your others will remain active.
Can a family member register themselves for events?
Only if you gave them their own login. If they don't have a login, you'll need to register them - but don't worry, it's easy! Just use the MECA ID switcher to select their ID, then register for the event normally.
Can family members see each other's results?
As the master account holder, you can see everyone's results by switching MECA IDs. Family members with their own login can only see their own results.
What if I want to give a member a login later?
Contact MECA support - they can convert a managed member to one with their own login.
Can a linked member compete without me being there?
Absolutely! They have their own MECA ID and can compete at any event independently. If they have their own login, they can also register themselves. If not, you just need to register them in advance.
