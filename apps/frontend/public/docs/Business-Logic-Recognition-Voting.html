Business Logic: Recognition & Voting

How the platform celebrates achievements: Hall of Fame, Forever Members, World Records, season-end Finals Voting, and World Finals.

Hall of Fame

Overview
Manually-curated honored members with photo, induction year, and citation.
Technical
hall_of_fame table with FK to profile (or just text fields if the honoree predates the platform). Public Hall of Fame page lists by induction year.
Why
Some honorees were inducted before the platform existed and don't have profiles. Allowing free-text honorees keeps the historical record intact.
Manage
/admin/hall-of-fame. Hall of Fame guide.

Forever Members

Overview
Lifetime membership granted to a curated list of contributors.
Technical
forever_members table separate from active memberships. The forever flag is informational — the active membership is still tracked in memberships with a special long-duration type.
Why
Forever status is distinct from "active membership" — a forever member who hasn't logged in for two years is still a forever member. Modeling them separately makes that semantic explicit.

/admin/forever-members. Forever Members.

SPL World Records

Overview
Curated list of verified world records by class.
Technical
spl_world_records with class, score, holder profile, event, date. Public page sorts by class, score descending.
Why
Records aren't auto-derived from results because verifying a world record requires manual confirmation (sealed equipment, clean run, no contestation). Manual curation guarantees data quality.

Finals Voting

Overview
Season-end member vote on awards (e.g. Spirit Award, Dueling Demos Champion).
Technical
Sessions go through Creating → Pending → Live → Completed. Plus two off-cycle states: Disabled (suspended), Canceled (admin-aborted). Members vote once per session via the public homepage card. Admin finalizes and publishes results.
Why
Six-state machine (with the two off-cycle states) means admins can pause a live session for emergency edits without ending it, and cancel test sessions cleanly. Finalize is one-way — once published, results are public and immutable.

/admin/finals-voting. Finals Voting guide.

World Finals & Pre-Registration

Overview
Season-end championship event with its own pre-registration flow including t-shirt sizing and hotel info.
Technical
Configuration page in admin defines dates, hotel info, t-shirt size availability, custom labels. Members pre-register through a member-facing page; their selections (size, extras) save to world_finals_preregistrations.
Why
WF has more logistics than a normal event (travel, lodging, swag) so it gets a richer pre-registration form. Modeling it as a separate workflow (rather than overloading event registration) keeps the WF complexity isolated.
