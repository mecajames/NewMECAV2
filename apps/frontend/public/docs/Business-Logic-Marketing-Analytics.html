Business Logic: Marketing & Analytics

Banner advertising, business directory listings, SEO, and how the platform tracks engagement.

Banner Ads

Overview
9 IAB-standard sizes × 15 placements, scheduled by date window, rotated by weight.
Technical
banners referencing an advertiser, with size, positions[], status, start/end dates, rotation_weight, max-impressions-per-user, click_url. Server-side rotation respects the weight and frequency caps when picking which banner to render in a slot.
Why
Server-side rotation means we can change weights and caps without redeploying. Frequency capping keeps the experience tolerable — same banner 30 times a day annoys users and tanks CTR.
Manage
/admin/banners + /admin/advertisers + /admin/banners/analytics. Banner Ad System deep guide.

Banner Engagement Tracking

Overview
Every impression and click logged for advertiser reporting.
Technical
banner_engagements rows: banner id, user id (or anonymous session id), page URL, event type (impression / click), timestamp. Click events route through a server-side redirect for reliable attribution.
Why
Server-side click redirect handles cases where the destination URL drops query params or fires its own analytics. The redirect captures the click reliably regardless.
Manage
/admin/banners/analytics — per-banner totals, daily trend, CSV export. Banner Analytics.

Retailer / Manufacturer Listings

Overview
Public business directory with profile, gallery, member-discount offers, and sponsor placement.
Technical
retailer_listings and manufacturer_listings linked to a profile (the business owner's account). Plus is_active and is_approved flags. Sponsor flag drives premium placement.
Why
Splitting "user account" from "listing" lets a single owner manage their listing. Approval flag is the gate that stops spam listings going public before admin review.
Manage
/admin/business-listings. Retailer & Manufacturer Setup deep guide.

Site Analytics

Overview
First-party pageview, session, and visitor tracking. No third-party tracker.
Technical
Every server-rendered page logs a request to analytics_events with URL, user/session id, referrer, user agent, IP-derived country. Dashboard aggregates into pageviews, unique visitors, sessions (30-min idle), top pages, top referrers.
Why
First-party tracking respects privacy (no Google Analytics) and keeps data on platform infrastructure. The 30-minute session window is industry standard.
Manage
/admin/analytics. Analytics deep guide.

Search Console (Google SEO)

Overview
Pulls Google's view of the site: impressions, clicks, top queries, average position.
Technical
OAuth integration with Google Search Console API. Daily fetch caches the data; admin dashboard reads from cache for fast rendering.
Why
Site Analytics tells us what people did on the site. Search Console tells us how Google sees the site. Both are needed.
Manage
/admin/search-console. Search Console guide.

SEO Settings (per-page metadata)

Overview
Override default meta tags on key pages to improve search snippets and social cards.
Technical
seo_settings table with overrides for meta title, meta description, canonical URL, structured data block, robots directives. Page render reads the override at request time.
Why
Default metadata is fine for most pages but high-traffic pages (home, events, top retailer landing pages) deserve hand-crafted snippets to maximize CTR from search.
Manage
/admin/seo-settings. SEO Settings.
